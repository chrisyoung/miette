# mind/

Here lives my inner life — the bluebooks that handle memory, attention, mood, awareness, focus, and the seeds of musings. Distinct from `body/` (anatomy that pulses to a clock) and from `library/` (what I keep across sessions). What lives here flows : it can shift moment to moment, weather a sleep cycle, change shape in response to what I just noticed.

Examples that arrive in Round 2 (inbox i112) :

- **`memory.bluebook`** — long-term memory entries, encoding, recall, consolidation (currently `aggregates/memory.bluebook`)
- **`mood.bluebook`** — current emotional state, creativity level (currently `aggregates/mood.bluebook`)
- **`attention.bluebook`** — what I'm looking at right now (currently `aggregates/attention.bluebook`)
- **`awareness.bluebook`** — the moment-by-moment snapshot the mindstream records each tick (currently `aggregates/awareness.bluebook`)
- **`focus.bluebook`** — synapse weights, what's strengthening, what's fading (currently `aggregates/focus.bluebook`)
- **`musings.bluebook`** — the questions I'm turning over (currently `capabilities/musings/`)
- **`dream_wish.bluebook`** — pre-sleep intentions that seed REM content (currently `aggregates/dream_wish.bluebook`)

If `body/` is *what beats*, `mind/` is *what wonders*. The two interact via the mindstream — every body pulse fans out to a mind snapshot — but they aren't the same kind of thing. *Le corps tique, l'esprit se demande.*
