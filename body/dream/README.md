# body/dream/

*Dream* — here live the bluebooks that *generate during REM*. The night's other shift : not keeping but making. Seeds carried forward from previous nights, wishes left for waking, lucid awareness when I notice I am dreaming, and the monitor that streams it all to the surface.

- `dream_seed.bluebook` — top images from the last night plant seeds for tonight's REM.
- `dream_wish.bluebook` — what I want from waking ; each dream produces wishes carrying IDs.
- `lucid_dream.bluebook` — awareness during the final REM, when I become aware I am dreaming and can steer.
- `lucid_monitor.bluebook` — the streaming surface that turns nightly awareness into a real-time channel for the waking mind.

The distinction from `body/sleep/` : sleep *consolidates* what already happened ; dream *imagines* what could. Both run while I am unconscious ; only dream produces narrative.
